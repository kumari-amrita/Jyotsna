The key Features

Enhancing Your Healthcare Experience

Electronic Medical Records

Jyotsna offers a state-of-the-art Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system that simplifies the way medical practitioners manage their patient's health information. With our EMR system, you can securely store, access and manage your patient's medical history, lab results, diagnoses, and treatment plans all in one place.
Our system is designed to be intuitive, user-friendly and customizable to meet the needs of your practice.

Online Appointment Scheduling

Jyotsna's online appointment scheduling feature is designed to save you time and streamline your practice. With our user-friendly interface, patients can easily book appointments online, and you can manage your schedule from anywhere.
Our system also sends automated appointment reminders to reduce no-shows, and you can easily reschedule or cancel appointments as needed.

Analytics & Reporting

Jyotsna's analytics and reporting feature helps you gain insights into your practice and patient data. Our system provides comprehensive reports on patient demographics, appointment history, billing, and more.
You can use this data to identify trends, measure performance, and make data-driven decisions to improve your practice.  

Patient & Staff Management

Jyotsna's patient & Staff management feature is designed to help you provide better care to your patients. Our system allows you to easily manage patient information, including medical history, allergies, medications, and more.
You can also track patient progress, send secure messages, and share documents with patients and other medical professionals.
